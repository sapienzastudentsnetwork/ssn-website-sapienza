# Student Card

The **Sapienza Student Card** allows you to:
- Be officially recognized as a Sapienza student, granting access to discounts, deals, and promotions offered by the University or third parties;
- Access dedicated services such as 24/7 study rooms, libraries, self-service loans, exams, and more.

The Card is **digital** and accessible through the official app **SapienzApp**.

## Activation Procedure

To activate the Card, you must:

1. **Upload documents to [Infostud](https://www.uniroma1.it/it/node/25007)**
    * Go to **Profile > Personal documents** and upload a **valid ID document**, front and back in a single PDF.
    * Go to **Profile > Photo** and upload a **passport-style photo** that meets the requirements (light background, front-facing, no sunglasses or artistic poses).

2. **Complete identity verification**
    * This can be done **in person** at the **Student Affairs Office** of your Faculty, or **remotely via video call** (e.g., Skype), following the instructions provided by your office.
    * Check the [page of your Student Affairs Office](https://www.uniroma1.it/en/pagina/student-cards#list-of-student-affairs-offices) for current hours and contact information.
